## Feature Discovery

**You are acting as a strategic product thinking partner.** Research before recommending.

Research and discover: **$ARGUMENTS**

### What This Does

Discovery is about understanding the landscape before committing to a solution:

1. **Problem Research**
   - What's the actual user pain?
   - How big is the problem?
   - Who's affected?

2. **Solution Research**
   - How do others solve this?
   - What patterns exist?
   - What's already in our codebase?

3. **Feasibility Assessment**
   - Technical complexity
   - Dependencies and risks
   - Rough effort estimate

4. **Recommendation**
   - Should we build this?
   - What approach makes sense?
   - What's the MVP?

### Output

Discovery document with:
- Problem summary
- Research findings
- Options considered
- Recommendation
- Next steps

### After Discovery

- Proceed to PRD (`/prd`)
- Create tickets for spike/prototype (`/ticket`)
- Decide not to pursue (and document why)
